# 📘 Admin Dashboard Documentation

The **Admin Dashboard** is the central control panel of the **iSoftware Lab Academy (School Management System and Online Learning Platform)**. It is designed to provide complete management and monitoring capabilities for administrators.

---

## 🔐 Access & Security
- **Secure Login System**: Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures only authorized admins can access sensitive operations.
- **Session Management**: Secure handling of administrator sessions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview (Home)
The homepage displays key analytics for quick decision-making using cards, charts, and graphs:
- **Total Students**: count of registered learners.
- **Active Enrollments**: current student-course associations.
- **Total Courses**: number of courses available on the platform.
- **Completed Quizzes**: assessment throughput.
- **Certificates Issued**: total recognitions granted.
- **Revenue**: financial tracking of course purchases.
- **Recent Activities**: real-time log of system events.

---

## 👥 Student Management
Provides granular control over student accounts:
- **Operations**: View, add, edit, delete, suspend, and search student accounts.
- **Detailed Profiles**: Access to enrollment history, course progress, quiz performance, and payment details.

---

## 📚 Course Management
Full lifecycle management of educational content:
- **Operations**: Create, update, publish, unpublish, and remove courses (e.g., Python, MERN, Flutter, Java, Full Stack).
- **Rich Content Support**: 
    - Textual explanations
    - Interactive code blocks
    - High-quality images
    - Technical diagrams
    - PDF resources
    - Video lectures
- **Configuration**: Set course prices, categories, instructors, and access permissions.

---

## 📝 Quiz Management
Comprehensive assessment control:
- **Quiz Creation**: Define quizzes per module or course.
- **Question Bank**: Add and edit multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
- **Settings**: Define correct answers, set passing marks, configure timers, and randomize question order.

---

## 🎓 Certificate Management
Automated recognition system:
- **Templates**: Create and manage certificate designs.
- **Auto-Issuance**: Certificates are generated automatically upon successful completion of courses and quizzes.
- **Verification**: Assign unique Certificate IDs and maintain a verification system.
- **Downloads**: Generate downloadable PDF certificates for students.

---

## 💳 Payment & Enrollment
Financial and access monitoring:
- **Transaction History**: Monitor all course purchases.
- **Payment Verification**: Verify successful transactions and process refunds.
- **Manual Control**: Manually enroll or remove students from courses when necessary.

---

## 💬 Comments & Discussion Management
Community moderation tools:
- **Moderation**: Approve, edit, or delete student comments, doubts, and replies.
- **Thread Management**: Maintain healthy discussion threads within the learning platform.

---

## 📢 Notifications & Announcements
Communication hub:
- **Targeted Messaging**: Send course updates, quiz reminders, and certificate alerts.
- **General Announcements**: Broadcast messages to all students or selected groups.

---

## 📈 Reports & Analytics
Deep insights into platform performance:
- **Student Performance**: Tracking average scores and completion rates.
- **Popularity Metrics**: Identifying top-performing courses.
- **Financial Trends**: Revenue forecasting and historical data.
- **Success Rates**: Quiz performance and certification statistics.

---

## ⚙️ Advanced Administrative Features
- **Instructor Management**: Manage teacher/instructor profiles and permissions.
- **Website CMS**: Control landing page content and site-wide configurations.
- **Attendance Monitoring**: Track student engagement and activity.
- **Assignment Review**: Manage and grade student submissions.
- **Live Class Scheduling**: Coordinate real-time learning sessions.
- **User Role Management**: Fine-grained permission control.
- **Settings Configuration**: Global system preferences and API integrations.
